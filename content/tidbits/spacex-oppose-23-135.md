EchoStar asked the FCC to reverse a waiver the agency's bureaus had granted for SpaceX's
satellite-to-phone service. SpaceX's reply, filed April 23, 2025, was blunt. EchoStar's
argument, it wrote, "ignores the very purpose of a waiver—which is to authorize the deviation
from a rule in appropriate circumstances." SpaceX asked the Commission to deny the appeal and
to "remove any cloud over SpaceX's ongoing deployment."

The waiver underpins the direct-to-cell service SpaceX is building with T-Mobile. The dispute is
one of 125 filings in docket 23-135.
